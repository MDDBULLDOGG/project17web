About Us

Bringing a special experience to VR

Project 17 is a fan-driven project inspired to recreate iconic experiences from Valve Software's Half-Life 2, rebuilt for VR. We're committed to bringing fans an immersive experience while providing the community with VR-ready assets for independent use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are you remaking Half-Life 2?

A: No. We believe the Half-Life: Alyx modding scene is still in its infancy. As of now, there are many tools that the community does not have access to that would be required to create a truly faithful recreation of Half-Life 2 that is enjoyable for the player.

Q: If you aren't recreating Half-Life 2, what are you recreating?

A: Project 17 at this stage is committed to recreating the "Point Insertion" chapter of Half-Life 2, while periodically releasing assets to the community for use in 3rd party maps and mods, in collaboration with the Half-Life 2: Remade Assets team.

Q: When will Project 17 release?

A: When it's ready™.
